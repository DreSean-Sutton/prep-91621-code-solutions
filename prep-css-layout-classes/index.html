Raiders of the Lost Ark

The start of an epic journey directed by Steven Spielberg and starred by Harrison Ford as Indiana Jones. Jones is tasked by the army with finding the Ark of the Covenant before Hitler, as he believes it can make his army invincible.

Temple of Doom

A prequal to the award winning Raiders of the Lost Ark. After surviving several murder attempts in 1935, Indiana Jones and his two companions find themselves on a quest to find and return 5 Sankara stones that were stolen from the mayapore village.

The Last Crusade

The third and final installment to a trilogy that has touched many hearts. Indiana Jones now searches for his missing father and the Holy Grail. in a breathtaking adventure around Europe.
